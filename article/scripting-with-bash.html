  • In a typical home network setup, the IP address range "192.168.1.0" to "192.168.1.255" is a private IP address range reserved for home networks. The IP address "192.168.1.10" is part of this private range, and it is most likely the local IP address assigned to your Mac by your home router.

  • "192.168.1.10" is your Mac's IP address on your home network. It is not the public IP address assigned by your Internet Service Provider (ISP), which is the address used to identify your home network on the internet. The public IP address is assigned to your router and is used for communication between your home network and the internet. The private IP address "192.168.1.10" is used for communication between devices within your home network.
